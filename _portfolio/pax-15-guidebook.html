PAX ’15 Guidebook

This conceptual project took the amazing event PAX Prime and overhauled its identity to create a printed guidebook for distribution at the event. On tabloid sized spreads I created all the necessary maps and one day's full schedule as well as general event information using PAX Prime 2014's official publications and the Washington State Convention Center's floor plans.

All body copy, the PAX Prime event and certain other materials are the sole property of Penny Arcade, Inc.
