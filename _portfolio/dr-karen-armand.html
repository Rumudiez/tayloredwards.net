Dr. Karen Armand

drkarenarmand.com

I helped Dr. Karen Armand transition her professional psychotherapy and yoga therapy services to a more personalized business which would greet clients with more intimacy than her previous web presence.

For this project I created a visual identity for Dr. Armand including a logo, color scheme, and type treatment which I then brought to form in this website. I was solely responsible for all aspects of this project from concept to implementation.

Tools

  • Development with Sublime Text 2 for WordPress
